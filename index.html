# AlmaLinux Mirrors

Mirrors are extremely important to provide fast, reliable infrastructure. We are very grateful to all of the AlmaLinux Mirror Operators, people and organizations that have setup and maintain mirrors.

AlmaLinux would like to personally thank HiVelocity (opens new window) for their sponsorship of the AlmaLinux OS Foundation and sponsorship of our primary rsync mirror and bandwidth. Please support those that support the community. Check out HiVelocity (opens new window) for all your dedicated server, bare metal, colo, CDN and hosting needs.

This page contains a list of official AlmaLinux (opens new window) mirrors. If you want to set up a public mirror, please read our mirror guidelines (opens new window). You can find the list of links to ISOs for all of mirrors here (opens new window).
